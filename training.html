Sophos Training

CS Solutions, Inc. collaborated with Mart-Networks Limited to provide the first ever Sophos Certified Administrator Training in Monrovia, Liberia. This training attracted Sophos XG Firewall users from the financial sector (banks), various institutions of government and the private sector. The certified trainer from Mart-networks Nigeria Ltd. conducted a 3 days hands-on training for 21 participants. Each participant was registered to sit the Certified Sophos XG Administrator external exam.
